The Outbound Diagnosis

Outbound moves 315 accounts through the funnel but only converts 5% of them to meetings. Downstream rates (35% Meetings→SQL, 67% SQL→SQO) are within normal range. The problem is first-meeting booking, not deal qualification. SDR leadership owns the April 20 diagnostic: is this volume, targeting, sequencing, messaging, or rep concentration?

The Product Case

Product converts meetings to SQLs at 38% (on par with Inbound/Outbound), and SQLs to SQOs at 100% (best in class). Average deal size $8,743 — higher than Inbound or Outbound. 832% PQL generation means the top-of-funnel supply is strong. Product looks like the strongest single investment lever we have.

Recommendation for Q2 Mid-Point Review

Treat Q2 as a measurement quarter: if Product SQO volume holds above 75% of Q1's rate in April + May, we should propose a Q2 mid-point investment shift. If it's a one-quarter aberration, rebalance toward Outbound fix.
