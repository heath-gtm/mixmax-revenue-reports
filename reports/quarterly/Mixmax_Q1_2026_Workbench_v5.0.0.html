6.3Root Cause Hypotheses

  • H1 (STRONGEST): Timing. 7 of 9 SQLs created in last two weeks of the month. January produced 0 SQLs from 61 PQLs. End-of-month sweeps, not continuous triage.
  • H2 (STRONG): Rep concentration. Karan 6, Isabelle 2. No triage layer between raw PQL and AE inbox.
  • H3 (MODERATE): Signal mix. 5 of 9 SQLs are Convert SS→DS (up-sell); 4 are cold Signups. Different motions, pooled.
  • H4 (MODERATE): Definition leak. 179 PQLs but only 49 Engaged accounts — signal-level count inflated by multi-signal accounts.
  • H5 (MODERATE): Qualification, not execution. SQO → Closed Won at 11% (1/9); BuilderTREND sat 2 months in Discovery.

(source: PQL Autopsy Q1)
