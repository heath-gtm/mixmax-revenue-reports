The TOFU Problem — Where to Invest for Q2

The pipeline shortfall is not uniform. It is concentrated in two specific failure modes that deserve different interventions.

Problem 1: Outbound is broken (17% of target on accounts engaged)
  • Outbound engaged 315 accounts against a 1,890 target — 1,575 accounts short.
  • Outbound Meetings Booked: 17 (vs 72 target, 24% of plan) · Outbound SQLs: 6 (vs 36, 17%) · Outbound SQOs: 4 (vs 21, 19%).
  • Outbound Pipeline $: $20,136 (vs $251,708 target, 8% of plan).
  • Where to solve: SDR capacity and/or SDR motion — we either don't have enough bodies or the plays aren't landing. This is the single biggest Q1 driver and the single biggest Q2 intervention.
Problem 2: Inbound MQL supply is soft (56% of target)
  • Inbound Accounts Engaged at 67% of plan, Inbound MQLs at 56%.
  • Inbound pipeline actually held up better at 44% of target $ — the inbound engine is converting fine, we just need more feed.
  • Where to solve: Demand gen spend + content/SEO. Marketing-owned.
Problem 3: Product PQLs are a bright spot being under-harvested
  • PQLs at 818% of target (180 vs 22) and Product pipeline at 414% of target $ ($79K vs $19K).
  • But Product SQLs are only 9 and Product SQOs only 9 — the conversion from PQL to SQL is tiny relative to the volume.
  • Where to solve: Build a dedicated Product-qualified motion — the raw signal is strong; the hand-off to sales is not.
