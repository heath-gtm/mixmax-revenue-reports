Top Cross-Account Findings

1. Salesforce Integration is universally absent — 0 of 7 accounts have any SF activity. This is the single biggest expansion theme. Every CSM conversation should include SF integration.
2. Dialer is nearly untouched — Only CivilGrid (1 call) and Small Batch (3 calls) have any Dialer usage. 5 of 7 accounts have zero calls placed.
3. Email Tracking usage is uneven — nirvanatech (42K views) and Landmark Global (4.6K) are power trackers, while Small Batch Standard has only 64 tracking views despite sending 2,400 emails.
4. Meeting Copilot shows promising traction — 5 of 7 accounts have Copilot activity. Landmark Global (20 recordings, 30 summaries) is the standout. This feature has upsell potential.
5. Sequences vary widely — Small Batch Standard (315 interactions) is a power user, nirvanatech is active (422), but MagicLinks and Agile Cloud have zero sequence activation despite thousands of emails sent.

Winning Playbook — Keep / Stop / Start

KEEP

  • Boomerang motion is the #1 win engine — 41% of ARR came from returning customers. The product loyalty/muscle memory is real. Keep nurturing former customer relationships.
  • Inbound is dominant — 62% of ARR from Inbound. The brand/product reputation is generating pipeline.
  • Speed to Value plays — 2-day closes (MagicLinks, Small Batch) show that urgent need + fast onboarding = signed deals.
  • Morgan Wible's volume — 9 of 12 deals (63% of ARR). Consistent execution across channels.

STOP

  • Closing non-ICP accounts without adoption plans — 5 of 12 deals (49% of ARR) are at risk post-close. Need qualification criteria or mandatory onboarding programs.
  • Single-threading deals — Enformion (1 user active) was single-threaded with the champion only.

START

  • Mandatory onboarding for deals >$5K — WeWork India ($5.9K, zero usage), Get Covered ($15K, went silent), Chicory ($16K, went silent). These needed structured onboarding.
  • Post-close health checks at 30/60/90 days — Early intervention could save the $42K at-risk ARR.
  • ICP scoring integration — Aero Low/Low accounted for 47% of ARR. Either recalibrate Aero or use it to trigger mandatory onboarding for off-ICP wins.
  • Expand Isabelle + Karan — Both closed the largest deals ($16K, $15K). Give them more at-bats.
