Key Takeaways

  • Competitive loss is the structural story. Outreach / Gong Engage / Salesloft together took 62% of PCV. Attentive Mobile was 51% of total on its own.
  • M&A is the second largest bucket. 19% of PCV ($62K) across 5 acquired accounts — Onfido (Entrust), WorkRamp, Alteia, namogoo, Ansel Health.
  • Consolidation + Self-Serve together = 14%. $31K consolidated stacks + $13K MtM downsizes represent contract-flexibility churn Mixmax can counter.
  • Pure product-gap churn was small. Only Pearl ($1.7K) cited explicit feature gaps, but Instacart's narrative surfaced SFDC-sync scale caps and LinkedIn/sequencing asks — product signal worth logging.
